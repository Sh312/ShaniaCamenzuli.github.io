about me


hii, i am Shania Camenzuli.


I am currently reading for a Bachelor of Fine Arts (Honours) in Digital Arts at the University of Malta.

Art allows me to express myself freely, to look for detail and to think creatively. I seek to communicate narratives through artistic means and where possible, imply a deeper meaning.

I specialise in illustration and and I am able to tackle projects holistically. Throughout these past three years, I created a body of work varying from illustration, photography, visual identity, packaging design and other design concepts.
