graphic design

delthea


This project delves into the design of a visual identity for a new brand and product. The design and the concept in general fuses the Maltese and the Greek cultures together. The brand and its visual identity are derived from the shape of the product itself (Phyllo Triangles). The brand values are authenticity, timelessness and precision.
