photography

spirit of the place


The intention of this documentation was to visually convey the atmosphere of this place at Vittoriosa. Having an eye for detail, I looked for certain features that differ this street from another. The textures, the colours, and the serenity that there is within, are communicated effectively through these photos.




checkmate at Matluwa Lane


The following is a sequential narrative where the frames are read chronologically from left to right, top to bottom. This narrative remarks the presence in absence. No individuals are physically present yet, there are marks of humanity naming the shadows, the mark on the seat and the chess being played.




fil-fond


This editorial publication documents the typical life of my grandparents who both live alone. Through this series of photographs, I witnessed their interactions and their way of living while getting to know them inside-out. By accompanying them throughout the day, I could conclude what the interior and the exterior spaces mean to them and why they seek to spend their time in that specific space.
